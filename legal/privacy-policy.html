Privacy Policy

CloseTool · Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

CloseTool (the "Application") is a private, internally-developed accounting workflow tool built and operated by Healthcare Markets DBA ("we," "us," "our") for its own internal month-end close process. The Application is not offered to the public and is used exclusively by Healthcare Markets DBA personnel.

2. Information We Access

The Application connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 API and accesses:

  • General ledger accounts, balances, and chart of accounts
  • Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow reports
  • Transaction-level detail for reconciliation and variance analysis

The Application only retrieves financial data from the QuickBooks company explicitly authorized by Healthcare Markets DBA. No personal, customer, or vendor personally identifiable information (PII) is collected, displayed, or stored beyond what is already maintained in the authorized QuickBooks Online company file.

3. How We Use the Information

Accessed financial data is used solely to:

  • Generate internal management reports (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Track and coordinate month-end close tasks among authorized accounting staff
  • Support reconciliation and variance commentary workflows

We do not sell, lease, share, or otherwise disclose accessed data to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Cached report data and internal workflow records are stored in a local SQLite database on devices controlled by Healthcare Markets DBA. OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally and are used only to refresh data from QuickBooks Online. No data is transmitted to any server outside of Intuit's API endpoints.

5. Security

Access to the Application is restricted by per-user authentication. OAuth credentials are stored with standard file-system permissions on authorized devices. The Application uses TLS (HTTPS) for all communication with Intuit's servers.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Cached QuickBooks data is retained for internal reporting and audit purposes. Authorized personnel may request deletion of cached data or revoke the application's QuickBooks access at any time via Intuit's "Apps" settings in QuickBooks Online.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to authorized users prior to taking effect.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy or the Application, contact joe@healthcaremarkets.com.
